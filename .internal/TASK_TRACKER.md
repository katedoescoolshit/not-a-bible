## üìù Notes & Decisions

### Technical Decisions
- **Next.js**: Chosen for landing site (SEO, performance, ease of deployment)
- **Tailwind CSS**: Chosen for styling (rapid development, consistency)
- **TypeScript**: Chosen for type safety and better DX
- **Vercel**: Chosen for hosting (free, fast, easy deployment)
- **KJV**: Chosen as first translation (public domain, widely used)

### Design Decisions
- **Dark theme**: Primary theme for landing page (urgency, seriousness)
- **Minimal design**: Focus on content, not decoration
- **Mobile-first**: Ensure mobile experience is excellent
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance minimum

### Content Decisions
- **Tone**: Urgent but not alarmist, serious but not preachy
- **Copy**: Direct, honest, emphasizes ownership and preservation
- **Messaging**: Focus on digital preservation as resistance
